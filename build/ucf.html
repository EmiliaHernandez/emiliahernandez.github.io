University of Central Florida - OSI Design Group

Web Designer, May 2021 - May 2023

I worked in a team of 2-3 web designers who were in charge of any site changes for 3 sites under the ucf domain. Most projects were completed collaboratively, but I chose the pages that I had the largest hand in designing/developing

Scroll over images :)

Pegasus Palooza

UCF's official welcome week has a micro-site dedicated to covering the events and sponsors.


  • Pegasus Palooza gets a new theme and brand guidelines each year, but as a team, we noticed that the website had a dull template and wanted to change this
  • The team got on Figma, proposed a new, fresher design to our supervisors, and we got approved to develop the pages
  • This page was designed by myself, and was later programmed collaboratively in Wordpress.
  • According to Google Analytics, the site recieved more than 100% higher pageviews, about 12% higher time on page, and 160% more entrances after all our work was complete and sent to the public

Knights of the Turntables Page

This page was requested to get a full redesign, and I was in charge of designing and developing it


  • KOT used to have 4 unique sections of the site, all with little content on each page, so I decided it was best to turn it into a one-pager
  • I designed sections that could easily be modified or removed if needed, such as the upcoming events.
  • I utilized Bootstrap, HTML/CSS, and PHP to create this page

Graphic Design

At one point in the semester, the office's dedicated graphic designers had too many requests to complete timely. I helped out and made some flyers


While my job wasn't to create graphic designs, I did have a strong love for illustration, and took this opportunity to make some flyers and to take advantage of the Adobe Creative Suite, which the graphic designers had access to.


During this time, I practiced using Illustrator, and this ultimately led to me designing signage, and a mural for the campus. I'm very grateful to have been given the access to that technology, and take my art to larger, more permanent places

Student Goverment Initiative Tracker

I creatively displayed the progress of that year's student initiatives


  • I met with SG staff and discussed some options - such as using the 3 school colors as progress meters
  • Students could follow the key above, or read more details about initiatives by clicking on the titles
