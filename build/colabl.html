CoLabl

Graphic and Digital Design Intern, Apr. 2024 - May 2024

The Taco Bell Foundation has a unique app designed for winners of the Live Mas Scholarship. I am part of this community and had the opportunity to propose designs that would be used for the app Live Mas Scholar Connect(LMSC).

Scroll over images :)

Appstore Screenshots

The task was to modernize the screenshots that would be placed in the app's page.


  • My design process was to heavily use the assets provided, such as the gradients, and sparkle vectors.
  • Next, I entered the app and fetched the screenshots that displayed core functionalities, and I created phone mockups.
  • While this design was not chosen as the one to move forward, elements were used in the final product - such as my layouts and photo editing.

Webpage Redesign

When users enter the app/webapp, the Guide page is the first thing they see, and the task was to moderize it as well.


  • This was my first iteration. Admittedly, the laptop I used to design was not color-calibrated too well, so this impacted design decisions.
  • Once again, any gradients that were provided were used in this proposal, but I wanted the design to breathe, so it was mostly white, with the gradients as support.
  • Out of the 4 interns I was working with, my design was selected to move forward, with some edits.

The Guide - Final Version

This is the version that was handed off to the developers to code.


  • This was my first time designing something that was handed over to other devs, and I had to change some of my original ideas due to resources.
  • I opted to make some sections full-width, and solid-colored, to ensure enough contrast with text.
  • I also listened to suggestions to include more photography of the scholars themselves, and I believe this made for a large improvement.
